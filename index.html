The Atelier

From a small atelier in Lebanon

No factory, no middle hands. Every piece is designed, set, and finished in one studio — and signed off before it ever reaches you.

“Gold has a memory — it keeps the warmth of your hand and the marks of the tools. I work slowly, alone, mostly. I'd rather make a few pieces I can stand behind than many I can't. When you reach out, you're talking to the person who will actually make your piece.” — The Maker
